Consent to Receive Notices by Email

By submitting this application, you confirm that if your application is successful and you become a tenant, you consent to receive all legally required notices and documents by email. This includes rent increase notices, the "How to Rent" guide, the Gas Safety Certificate, the Energy Performance Certificate, and the Prescribed Information relating to any government-approved tenancy deposit scheme. The email address you provide in this application will be treated as valid for the purpose of serving these notices and documents.

Holding Deposit – Important Notice

By submitting this application, you agree to pay a holding deposit equivalent to one week's rent to reserve the property.

In accordance with the Tenant Fees Act 2019, the holding deposit will be refunded or applied to the tenancy agreement, except in the following circumstances where it may be withheld:

  • You provide false or misleading information
  • You fail a Right to Rent check
  • You withdraw from the property
  • You fail to take reasonable steps to enter into the tenancy

Important: If false or misleading information is provided unintentionally or is not material, the deposit will not automatically be forfeited.

Examples of False or Misleading Information:

  • Claiming a higher income than earned
  • Falsifying employment or landlord details
  • Omitting poor credit history (e.g. CCJs, bankruptcy)
  • Providing fake references
  • Not disclosing occupants
  • Misrepresenting nationality or immigration status
  • Providing false information regarding Right to Rent status

10. All Additional Occupants (Adults and Children)

Note: Each adult applicant (18+) who will be on the tenancy agreement must complete a separate form and will be subject to referencing and Right to Rent checks as required by UK law. Children under 18 will be listed as permitted occupiers and do not need to be on the tenancy agreement.
